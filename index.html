My Background / About Me
I'm a second-year master student currently studying Quantitative Methods at Vanderbilt University based in Nashville, TN. At the same time I am also part of the Evaluation and Assessment Team of our medical school program focusing on doing data analysis and database management for medical students and educators. I had two years of experience handling different kinds of data using Python, R and SQL.
My main interest and focus are finding solutions for data-driven decisions, by using techniques such as Exploratory Data Analysis, Data Visualization, and Machine learning models to explore data insights hidden in different datasets. During the past few years, I have had the chance to work and research in different areas such as Education, Healthcare, and Social Sciences. I have participated and lead several projects and I am looking forward to further opportunities in my next career step.
Being a curious person, so I am always interested in learning new skills and engaging new hobbies in my spare time. I love creating novel things by painting and composing music. I am also interested in telling story using photography especially capturing landscape views.
